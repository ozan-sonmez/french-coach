🇫🇷 French Pronunciation Practice (Starter App)

A lightweight web app to practice **French pronunciation** directly from your phone or browser.

This project was created as a simple **pronunciation training tool** for daily practice.  
It allows learners to **listen to sentences, record their voice, and repeat pronunciation exercises**.

The app runs entirely in the browser — no installation required.

---

# ✨ Features

- 🔊 Listen to French sentences (normal and slow speed)
- 🎙 Record your own pronunciation
- 🔁 Repeat sentences as many times as needed
- ⭐ Self-evaluate your pronunciation
- 📝 Add personal notes
- 📱 Mobile-friendly interface
- 💾 Progress stored locally in your browser

# 📱 How to Use

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Select a sentence category
3. Listen to the sentence
4. Record your voice
5. Compare your pronunciation
6. Repeat and improve

Tip:  
On iPhone, you can add the app to your home screen:

Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen
